Our Mission

Lifting Life empowers foster youth and underrepresented individuals to build stable, flourishing lives. Through mentorship, education, and career-readiness programs, we help them plant the seeds of their future and thrive in the AI-driven era.

Our Pathway to Independence

We guide our members along a three-stage journey, with mentorship as the constant thread weaving it all together. Every step is designed to build confidence, capability, and community.

STAGE 1

Education

We guide our members in discovering their interests and securing scholarships, while building practical skills through workshops in English.

STAGE 2

Stability Through Work

We move members toward independence through literacy support, vocational training, and job readiness skills for apprenticeships and college.

STAGE 3

AI-Era Readiness

We prepare members for long-term success with financial literacy, digital skills, career coaching, and guidance toward home ownership and self-reliance.
